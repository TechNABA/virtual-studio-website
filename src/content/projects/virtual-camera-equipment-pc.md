- The first version was an iPad Pro with a cover and two controllers with the integrated Live Link VCam app
- The Virtual Camera has been significantly upgraded to a device that displays images up to 4K with zero latency
- The equipment mounts a Teradek Bolt 4K LT 750 video receiver connected to an Atomos Shinobi monitor via SDI
- The latest upgrade was to integrate two Nintendo JoyCon controllers to change the values of the CineCameraActor in real-time
