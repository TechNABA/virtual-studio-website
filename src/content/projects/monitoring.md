Monitoring video sources is crucial when it comes to addressing and studying the signal. We have added several devices in the studio to facilitate this task:

**CONTROLLERS**
- Roland V-160HD Video Switcher
- 8x8 HDMI 18G Matrix with Video Wall Scaling
- Front panel HDMI/SDI Patches

**MONITORS**
- Atomos Sumo 19"
- Flanders DM240
- LC C3 OLED
