The **Brainbar** is the centre of operation of every virtual production set, and we tried to build it in such a way that it was as ergonomic as possible. The custom desk with aluminium profiles allows us to add and remove new components with ease.

**WORKSTATION 01 – UNREAL OPERATOR & STREAMING**
- CPU: 2X Intel Xeon Silver 4208 2.1GHz (3.2GHz Turbo, 8Core)
- RAM: 128GB (8x16GB DDR4 2933 Mhz)
- SSD: 4X M.2 1TB PCIe NVMe
- GPU: Nvidia RTX A5000, 24GB
- Declink 8K Pro

**WORKSTATION 02 – CAMERA TRACKING**
- CPU: Intel Core i7-12700K processor (12 Core , 3.6GHz to 5.0GHz)
- RAM: 32GB, 2x16GB, DDR5 up to 4400MHz
- SSD: 1TB PCIe NVMe M.2
- GPU: Nvidia RTXA4000, 16GB
