We developed a new type of Head head-mounted camera with two cameras for a stereoscopic type system. This allows us through integration with Metahuman Animator to achieve infinitely higher quality by creating depth maps of the face while the performance is taking place. Not only are we able to record the animation but also to create a 3D mesh from the video recording.

Hardware shortlist:

- 2x Custom 3D printed Camera cages
- 2x Wireless video signal
- 2x Aja Rack Recorder
