- A Multi-User server is used to enable real-time collaboration during the construction of environments. It is also used in the final stage of production to make changes on the set.
- The Multi-User server is hosted within a virtual machine on campus and is accessible via a VPN connection so that users from all over the world can connect to the session.
- Our aim was to facilitate the work of the director or DP, allowing them to visualise the camera movement or skeletal animations in real-time inside the blockout or the final environment.
